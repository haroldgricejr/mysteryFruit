Mystery Fruit

You'll be given four facts about a fruit, but a fact is revealed one at a time. If you're able to guess the fruit based on just one fact you get a total of five points. The point decreases by 1 whenever you need a second fact to guess the fruit. You only get 3 chances to guess per fact revealed, and you only get 30s to guess.
